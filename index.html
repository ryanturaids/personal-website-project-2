Hey, I'm Ryan!

I used to sell closets, now I'm looking forward to building applications and websites. I've spent most my life in California along with a brief stint in Texas, and while I am a homebody I occasionally have the urge to travel and explore. I enjoy using tech but have always hated the feeling of not understanding what's under the hood. For a long time I've been looking forward peeling back the curtain!

Why am I here?

I love solving problems and making those solutions self-correcting. In my career, I've spent a lot of time automating tasks at work. When these solutions got too complicated for me I tried teaching myself better methods, but quickly hit walls when anything beyond basic algebra and Excel-functions got involved. Lacking structure and trying to learn was like trying to decipher hieroglyphics in another language. I could always figure out chunks of how something worked, but never was able to figure out how to connect it all functionally. After realizing that I find more satisfaction in building and improving systems than in using them, I want to shift my career-path entirely. This is a ground floor for me, and with work secured in this field I plan on diving fully into every area of programming and development I can wrap my mind around.

"We are stuck with technology when what we really want is just stuff that works."
